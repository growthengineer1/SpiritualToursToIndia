Buddha Circuit Tour

Follow the Path of Enlightenment

10 Days / 9 Nights

Tour Overview

Trace the footsteps of Buddha through the most sacred sites of Buddhism. This transformative journey covers the four major pilgrimage sites associated with Buddha's life, teachings, and enlightenment.

From Lumbini where Buddha was born, to Bodh Gaya where he attained enlightenment, Sarnath where he gave his first sermon, and Kushinagar where he achieved Mahaparinirvana - experience the essence of Buddhist spirituality.

Tour Highlights

  • Meditate under the Bodhi Tree in Bodh Gaya
  • Visit the sacred Maya Devi Temple in Lumbini (Buddha's birthplace)
  • Experience Sarnath - where Buddha delivered his first sermon
  • Pay respects at Kushinagar where Buddha attained Parinirvana
  • Explore ancient Buddhist monasteries and international temples
  • Participate in guided meditation sessions with Buddhist monks
