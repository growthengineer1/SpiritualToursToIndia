Char Dham Yatra

Sacred Himalayan Pilgrimage

14 Days / 13 Nights

Tour Overview

Embark on India's most sacred pilgrimage to the four divine abodes nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas. The Char Dham Yatra is believed to wash away sins and lead to moksha (liberation).

This spiritual journey takes you to Yamunotri (source of Yamuna), Gangotri (source of Ganga), Kedarnath (abode of Lord Shiva), and Badrinath (abode of Lord Vishnu) amidst breathtaking Himalayan landscapes.

Important Requirements

This pilgrimage requires good physical fitness due to high-altitude trekking (up to 12,000 ft). Includes moderate to difficult treks and steep climbs. Best season: May to June and September to October.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit all four sacred dhams in the Himalayas
  • Bathe in holy thermal springs at Yamunotri
  • Witness the origin of River Ganga at Gangotri
  • Trek to the ancient Kedarnath Temple (Lord Shiva)
  • Darshan at Badrinath Temple with mountain backdrop
  • Experience traditional Hindu rituals at each sacred site
