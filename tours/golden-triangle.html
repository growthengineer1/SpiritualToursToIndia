Golden Triangle Spiritual Tour

Delhi • Agra • Jaipur

7 Days / 6 Nights

Tour Overview

Experience the spiritual essence of India's most iconic circuit. The Golden Triangle combines ancient temples, sacred rituals, and architectural marvels that have inspired seekers for centuries.

From Delhi's magnificent temples to Agra's spiritual sunset at the Taj Mahal, and Jaipur's sacred pink city temples, this journey offers a perfect introduction to India's spiritual heritage.

Tour Highlights

  • Witness sunrise prayers at Delhi's Akshardham Temple
  • Experience the spiritual energy of the Taj Mahal at sunset
  • Visit Jaipur's sacred Govind Dev Ji Temple for evening aarti
  • Explore ancient Mughal gardens and meditation spots
  • Participate in traditional Hindu rituals with English-speaking guides
