South India Temple Tour

Chennai • Madurai • Rameshwaram • Kanyakumari

12 Days / 11 Nights

Tour Overview

Journey through South India's magnificent temple architecture and living spiritual traditions. This comprehensive tour explores the Dravidian temple heritage that has flourished for over 2,000 years.

From the towering gopurams of Madurai to the sacred waters of Rameshwaram, experience the devotion, rituals, and architectural grandeur that make South India a spiritual powerhouse.

Tour Highlights

  • Witness the grand evening aarti at Madurai Meenakshi Temple
  • Holy dip at Rameshwaram's 22 sacred wells
  • Sunrise meditation at Kanyakumari - India's southernmost point
  • Explore UNESCO World Heritage temples of Mahabalipuram
  • Participate in traditional Tamil temple rituals with expert guides
