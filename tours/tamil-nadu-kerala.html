Tamil Nadu & Kerala Tour

Temples, Backwaters & Ayurveda

10 Days / 9 Nights

Tour Overview

Combine Tamil Nadu's architectural temple wonders with Kerala's tranquil spiritual wellness traditions. This unique tour balances ancient temple exploration with rejuvenating Ayurvedic experiences.

From Madurai's towering temples to Kerala's serene backwaters and healing Ayurvedic centers, experience the perfect blend of devotion and holistic wellness.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit Tamil Nadu's iconic Meenakshi and Brihadeeswara temples
  • Experience traditional Ayurvedic treatments at Kerala wellness centers
  • Sunset cruise on Kerala's peaceful backwaters
  • Yoga and meditation sessions in serene natural settings
  • Explore spice plantations and learn about Ayurvedic herbs
